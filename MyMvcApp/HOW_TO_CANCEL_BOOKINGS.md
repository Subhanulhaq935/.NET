## Overview
Both **Customers** and **Service Providers** can cancel bookings in SkillHub. This guide explains how to cancel bookings from both perspectives.

3. Find your booking in the **"Upcoming Bookings"** section

### When You Can Cancel
- âœ… **Pending** bookings - Can be cancelled anytime

### How to Cancel
1. In the **Upcoming Bookings** section, find the booking you want to cancel
2. Click the **"Cancel Booking"** button (red button with X icon)
3. Confirm the cancellation when prompted
4. The booking status will change to **"Cancelled"**
5. You'll see a success message confirming the cancellation

### What Happens After Cancellation
- The booking status changes to "Cancelled"
- The booking moves from "Upcoming Bookings" to "Past Bookings"
- The provider is notified (the booking status is updated in their dashboard)

### When You Can Cancel
- âœ… **Pending** bookings - Can be cancelled anytime
- âœ… **Accepted** bookings - Can be cancelled before completion
- âŒ **Completed** bookings - Cannot be cancelled
- âŒ **Already Cancelled** bookings - Cannot be cancelled again

### How to Cancel

#### From Dashboard:
1. In **Upcoming Bookings** section, find the booking
2. Click the **"Cancel"** button (red button with X icon)
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. Booking status updates to "Cancelled"

#### From Manage Bookings Page:
1. In the bookings table, find the booking row
2. In the **Actions** column, click **"Cancel"** button
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. Booking status updates to "Cancelled"

### Additional Actions Available
- **Accept** - Accept a pending booking (changes status to "Accepted")
- **Cancel** - Cancel a pending or accepted booking
- **Mark Completed** - Mark an accepted booking as completed (only after booking time has passed)

## â“ Troubleshooting

### "Only pending or accepted bookings can be cancelled"
- **Solution**: You're trying to cancel a booking that's already completed or cancelled. Check the booking status first.

### "Cannot cancel a booking that has already passed"
- **Solution**: The booking date/time has already passed. You can only cancel future bookings.

### Cancel button not showing
- **Check**: Make sure you're logged in as the correct user (Customer or Provider)
- **Check**: Verify the booking status is "Pending" or "Accepted"
- **Check**: Ensure the booking date/time hasn't passed
